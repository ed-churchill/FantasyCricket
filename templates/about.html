How Fantasy Cricket Works

Step 1: Allocation
  • Each UWMCC player is assigned a PRICE
  • Each UWMCC is assigned a ROLE (Batsman, Bowler, All-Rounder or Wicket-Keeper
  • Each UWMCC player is assigned a SQUAD (1s/2s, 3s/4s, or Development)
Step 2: Team Selection

Entrants select their Fantasy XI. Each team MUST:

  • Cost no more than Â£100m
  • Contain exactly 4 batsmen (keepers can also be played as batsmen)
  • Contain exactly 3 bowlers
  • Contain exactly 3 all-rounders
  • Contain at least 1 wicket-keeper (one of which is the designated keeper, the others are played as batsmen)
  • Contain no more than 6 players from the same squad
Step 3: Play!
  • BUCS matches are played each week
  • Individual players score points, contributing towards their team's overall points total (see below for how points are calculated)
  • Track the League Table and all the stats you need on this website!
